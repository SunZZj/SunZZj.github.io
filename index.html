Zheng Wang

 

I am currently a Post-doc Project Researcher in Satoh-Lab, National Institute of Informatics (NII), Japan, supervised by Prof. Shin'ichi Satoh. I obtained my Ph.D from Wuhan University in 2017. My Ph.D supervisor is Prof. Ruimin Hu, at National Engineering Research Center for Multimedia Software (NERCMS), School of Computer Science, Wuhan University. My research interests focus on person re-identification and instance search. I won the Best Paper Award at the 15th Pacific-Rim Conference on Multimedia (PCM 2014).
